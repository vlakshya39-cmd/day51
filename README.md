# 🎓 StudyPilot AI

StudyPilot AI is an AI-powered study companion designed for first- and second-year college students. It helps students organize their learning journey with personalized study roadmaps, daily task planning, progress tracking, and AI-guided learning support.

This project is being developed as the final capstone for the #60DayClaudeChallenge.

---

## ✨ Problem

Students often struggle with:

- Information overload from YouTube, documentation, AI tools, and online courses
- Not knowing what to study next
- Poor consistency and progress tracking
- Managing multiple learning resources

StudyPilot AI solves these problems by providing a personalized learning roadmap and intelligent study planning.
