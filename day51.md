# Day 51 — Product Discovery & Sprint Planning

## Objective

Kick off the 10-day capstone project by defining the product vision, refining the idea, and creating all planning documents.

---

## Project

**StudyPilot AI**

An AI-powered study companion that helps college students create personalized learning roadmaps, organize study resources, track progress, and maintain consistency.

---

## Deliverables

- ✅ Product Discovery Interview
- ✅ Approved Project Summary
- ✅ Product Requirements Document (PRD)
- ✅ Implementation Blueprint
- ✅ Project Pitch Deck

---

## Key Learnings

- Learned how product discovery shapes a software project before development begins.
- Understood the importance of defining the target audience and solving a real problem.
- Planned the project scope to keep Version 1 realistic within a 10-day timeline.
- Created structured documentation that will guide development over the coming days.
- Experienced how AI can assist in product planning, documentation, and sprint preparation.
